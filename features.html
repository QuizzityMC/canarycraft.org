ADSCRAFT Civilisations Features

Custom Civilisations

Create and grow your own unique civilization with custom mechanics. Choose from various government types, cultural focuses, and technological advancements to shape your society's destiny.

Dynamic Economy

Engage in a player-driven economy with supply and demand mechanics. Trade resources, establish trade routes, and build your civilization's wealth through commerce and industry.

Diplomacy System

Form alliances, declare wars, or maintain neutrality with other civilizations. Engage in diplomatic negotiations and influence the geopolitical landscape of the server.

Custom Building System

Unlock unique building styles and structures as your civilization advances. Create architectural wonders that reflect your civilization's culture and technological progress.

Tech Tree Progression

Research and unlock new technologies to advance your civilization. Progress through different eras, from ancient times to the future, unlocking new abilities and structures along the way.

Regular Events

Participate in server-wide events, from natural disasters to alien invasions. Work together or compete against other civilizations in these dynamic, world-altering occurrences.
