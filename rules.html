ADSCRAFT Civilisations Rules

1. Respect Other Players

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, bullying, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. No Griefing

Destroying or altering other players' builds without permission is strictly prohibited. This includes theft of resources and items.

3. No Cheating or Exploits

The use of mods, hacks, or any external tools that provide an unfair advantage is forbidden. Report any bugs or exploits to the staff immediately.

4. Appropriate Language and Behavior

Keep chat and behavior family-friendly. Excessive profanity, inappropriate topics, and spamming are not allowed.

5. Respect Server Events

Participate in server events in good faith. Intentionally disrupting events or exploiting event mechanics is prohibited.

6. Fair Play in Warfare

While inter-civilization conflicts are allowed, they must be conducted fairly. No spawn camping, excessive griefing during wars, or targeting inactive civilizations.

7. Use Official Channels for Support

For any issues, questions, or reports, use the official support channels on our Discord server or Canary Chat.
